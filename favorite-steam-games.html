Favorite Steam Games

Hello! This website showcases my top 5 favorite Steam games. It includes:

  1. A Dance of Fire and Ice
  2. Plants vs. Zombies: GOTY
  3. 64.0
  4. Lily's Well
  5. Spooky's Jump Scare Mansion

1. A Dance of Fire and Ice
Created by 7th Beat Games

A Dance of Fire and Ice is an amazing rhythm game and is super unique in it's nature. Instead of a VSRG where there are hit lines at the bottom of the screen where you hit the notes, you have 2 revolving planets that hit tiles to the beat. This game is insanely fun and addicting and I highly recommend that you try it out. 10/10.

2. Plants vs. Zombies: Game of the Year
Created by PopCap Games Inc.

Plants vs. Zombies is a classic game from the early 2010s. The main objective is simple: place plants down on your garden using sun energy to attack the zombies and stop them from entering your home. This gameplay is then twisted in many of it's different worlds that it has. Overall, PVZ is a really fun game. 10/10 game.

3. 64.0
Created by rebel rabbit

64.0, similarly to A Dance of Fire and Ice, takes a unique approach to rhythm that many games don't adopt. It uses bullets you can use to shoot at the "bad guys" to the rhythm, and let the good guys in. Overall, it's very unique and fun. 10/10 game overall.

4. Lily's Well
Created by PureIceBlue

Lily's Well is an RPG horror game that has an amazing storyline and amazing characters. The game follows a girl named Lily who is left home alone by her dad on a rainy night. She is able to collect many items to build a rope to go down into the well outside. There are many endings and many paths you can take in Lily's Well, and there's always something new to discover. Overall, an amazing 10/10 game.

5. Spooky's Jump Scare Mansion
Created by KIRA LLC

Spooky's Jump Scare Mansion is also a horror game that follows the player through a mansion of 1000 doors. Every 50 doors, theres an elevator that takes you to another floor of the mansion. Each door has something new, whether it be a monster, puzzle, or a regular room. Overall, this game is amazing and had me wondering what was to come next. 10/10 game.

Final Thoughts

These games are my top five favorite Steam games! I have linked all of them if you would like to play them! I hope you also find them fun as well :).
